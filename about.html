About Local Occupational Therapy

About Us

Local OT was formed by two dedicated and passionate therapists who have over 20 years combined experience practicing as Occupational Therapists. We have experience working for domestic and international public health organisations, disability services, the private sector and government services. Local OT was formed with the aim of combining local knowledge with high-quality Occupational Therapy services; to offer person and community centered care for each of our clients.

We are a mobile service and can arrange to meet you at your home, workplace or in the community.

Our OTs are registered with the Australia Health Practitioners Regulation Agency.

Local OT has a privacy and information management policy in place to ensure we offer a high quality service that protects your right to privacy, please contact us if you would like a copy. Local OT is a Registered NDIS and Medicare Provider.
